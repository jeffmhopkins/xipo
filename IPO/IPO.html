About & install

A private, offline-first tracker for SpaceX employees to model equity value and watch the SPCX listing. Works fully offline once loaded; your data never leaves this device.

Install: in Chrome/Edge use the install icon in the address bar (or menu → Install). On iOS Safari: Share → Add to Home Screen.

How the numbers work

  • Option net value = vested options × max(price − strike, 0)
  • RSU value = vested shares × price
  • Net proceeds = gross − exercise cost − estimated tax
  • Valuation → price = valuation ÷ shares outstanding
  • Vesting = stepped tranches after the cliff, per your schedule

Not affiliated with SpaceX. Estimates only; not financial, legal, or tax advice. Consult a qualified advisor. All public figures (valuation, share count, prices) are third-party reported and may be inaccurate — treat them as editable defaults.
